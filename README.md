# Informed Vote

An interactive app that helps voters make informed decisions in minutes, not hours.

## What it does

1. **Choose your election** — Select any election (US, local, international)
2. **Pick your issues** — Select and rank the issues that matter most to you
3. **Get unbiased comparison** — Claude searches real data and presents candidates' actual positions, voting records, and how each outcome impacts your life
4. **Find Your Voice** — Write your raw opinion and the app helps you turn it into a polished social media post, letter to your representative, dinner table argument, or personal essay

## Why it matters (Creative Flourishing)

Democracy is a form of creative expression. This app doesn't just inform — it empowers people to articulate and share their civic voice. By transforming messy, unformed opinions into powerful expression, Informed Vote bridges the gap between being informed and being heard.

## How Claude is used

- **Web Search**: Claude searches for real-time candidate positions and voting records
- **Analysis**: Claude processes and compares candidates' stances across user-prioritized issues
- **Creative Expression**: Claude transforms raw user opinions into polished content across multiple formats
- **Follow-up Q&A**: Users can ask deeper questions about any topic
